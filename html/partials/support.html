What is Open Scp1310jp1?
Open Scp1310jp1 is an open source, re-implementation of https://myscp1310jp1.com/. The frontend, html, css, JavaScript, were adapted from, and originally developed by https://myscp1310jp1.com/. The Open Scp1310jp1 backendis open source, free to use and modify.
